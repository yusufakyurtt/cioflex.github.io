Purchases and subscriptions

Premium features are handled through Apple in-app purchases and RevenueCat purchase infrastructure. If premium access is missing, open Momentoid, go to the Settings screen, and use Restore Purchases.

To manage, cancel, upgrade, or review your subscription, open the Settings screen and choose Subscriptions & Upgrades. That opens Apple's subscription management page.

Photos and permissions

Photos you capture or import are stored locally on your device for the app features you choose. Momentoid does not upload your photo journal memories to our servers.

Camera, photo library, and notification permissions are requested only when the related feature is used. On iOS, selecting a photo from the system photo picker may not require a full photo library permission prompt because the app receives only the photo you choose.

Quick answers

How do I restore purchases?

Open Momentoid, go to Settings, and tap Restore Purchases.

How do I manage or cancel subscriptions?

Open Settings in Momentoid and tap Subscriptions & Upgrades to open Apple's subscription management page.

Are my photos uploaded?

No. Your photo memories are designed to stay on your device unless you choose to share or save them through system features.

Why does the photo picker not ask permission?

iOS can let you choose a specific photo without giving the app access to your full library. This is expected privacy-friendly behavior.
