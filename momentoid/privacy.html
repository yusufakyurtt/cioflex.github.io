PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes what information Momentoid may collect or process, how that information is used, when it may be shared with service providers, and how you can contact us about your privacy rights. This policy is intended to support App Store disclosure requirements and should be read together with the privacy information shown in the app listing.

Last updated: June 24, 2026

1. Who we are

Momentoid is a mobile application for capturing photos, styling them with a polaroid-like presentation, and sharing them. If you have any privacy questions or requests, you can contact us at cioflexdev@gmail.com.

2. Information we may collect

Depending on the features you use, Momentoid may process the following categories of information:

  • Photos you capture or select, along with related local metadata required for storage and display.
  • Camera and photo library permission status so the app can access those features when you allow it.
  • Device, diagnostics, crash, and usage analytics information used to improve reliability and performance.
  • Notification-related technical identifiers and preference data if push notifications are enabled.
  • Purchase and entitlement validation data needed to unlock and restore premium features.
  • Support communications you send to us by email.

3. How we use information

  • To provide photo capture, framing, filter, saving, and sharing features.
  • To maintain app security, functionality, and performance.
  • To validate purchases, restore premium access, and prevent abuse.
  • To deliver notifications if you opt in.
  • To answer support, privacy, or account-related requests.

4. Permissions

Momentoid may request access to your camera and photo library. These permissions are used only to support core app features such as taking photos, selecting existing photos, and saving edited output. You can manage permissions at any time through your device settings.

5. Third-party services

Momentoid may use third-party providers for analytics, notifications, and purchase infrastructure, including services such as Firebase Analytics, OneSignal, and RevenueCat or equivalent providers used by the app at the time of release. These providers may process limited technical or transaction-related information on our behalf to support the service.

6. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers or platform partners when reasonably necessary to operate the app, process purchases, deliver notifications, maintain analytics, comply with legal obligations, or protect the rights, safety, and integrity of the service.

7. Storage and retention

Some content, including saved photos and settings, may be stored locally on your device. Other technical, analytics, notification, or purchase validation data may be processed through third-party infrastructure. Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer period is required by law or to resolve disputes.

8. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of certain personal information. You may also disable permissions or notifications in your device settings. Privacy and deletion requests can be sent to cioflexdev@gmail.com.

9. Children's privacy

Momentoid is not directed to children under the age at which parental consent is required in the relevant jurisdiction. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us and we will review the request.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with the updated revision date. Your continued use of the app after changes become effective indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, deletion requests, or App Store review-related contact needs, email cioflexdev@gmail.com.
