Privacy Policy

This page explains what types of data may be processed in Sakli Kelime and the purposes for which they may be used.

Data that may be collected

  • Account information: player identity, display name, and sign-in details
  • Gameplay data: guesses, results, streaks, score, and archive history
  • Social data: friends, duel invites, and completed match history
  • Technical data: app version and basic troubleshooting information

Why data may be used

  • Account creation and session management
  • Operating the daily game, duels, and social features
  • Showing score, profile, and progress information
  • Security, troubleshooting, and service reliability

Data retention and deletion

User data may be retained for as long as needed to provide the service. For account or data deletion requests, use the Account Deletion page.

Children's privacy

The app is intended for a general audience. If a parent or legal guardian wants to make a request regarding a child's data, the same account deletion and support channels may be used.
