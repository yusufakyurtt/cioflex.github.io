Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026. This Privacy Policy explains how Sakli Kelime may collect, use, store, and delete information when you use the app.

Information we may collect

  • Account-related information, such as player identifiers, display names, and sign-in details
  • Gameplay information, such as guesses, scores, streaks, archived results, and duel activity
  • Social information, such as friends, duel invites, and related match records
  • Technical information, such as app version and basic diagnostics needed to operate or support the app

How we may use information

  • To create and manage accounts and sessions
  • To provide gameplay, duel, social, and leaderboard features
  • To show profile, progress, and game history information
  • To maintain security, fix problems, and improve reliability
  • To respond to support, privacy, or deletion requests

Third-Party Services & Advertising

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, including but not limited to:

  • Advertising: We use third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob) to display advertisements. These networks may collect device identifiers and usage data to provide targeted personalized ads. On iOS, we will ask for your permission (App Tracking Transparency) before tracking your activity for personalized ads.
  • Analytics & Backend: We use services like Supabase and Google Firebase Analytics to manage accounts, databases, and track app usage and performance.
  • Purchases: We use RevenueCat to manage and validate in-app purchases. Your purchase history and a unique app user ID may be shared with RevenueCat to securely provide access to purchased features.
  • Push Notifications: If you opt-in, we collect push notification tokens to send you game alerts and updates. You can turn these off at any time in your device settings.

Data retention

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, maintain account features, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce service rules.

Your choices and requests

You may contact cioflexdev@gmail.com for support requests, privacy questions, or account deletion requests. Account deletion details are described on the Account Deletion page.

Children's privacy

Sakli Kelime is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, contact cioflexdev@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date on this page.
