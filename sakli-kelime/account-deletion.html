Account Deletion

If you want your Sakli Kelime account removed, follow the steps listed on this page.

Information needed for a request

  • Display name
  • The email address linked to the account, if available
  • Any additional detail that helps identify the correct account

Types of data that may be deleted

  • Account record
  • Profile and display name details
  • Score, archive, and gameplay history
  • Friend and duel-related records

How to submit a request

  1. Submit an account deletion request through the support channel connected to this page or through the app marketplace support method.
  2. Include the account-identifying details listed above.
  3. Additional verification information may be requested when necessary.

Note

Guest or local sessions can usually be removed by clearing app data on the device. For server-linked accounts, the formal request flow above should be used.
