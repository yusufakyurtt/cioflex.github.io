Account Deletion

Account Deletion

If you want your Sakli Kelime account and associated data deleted, send a request to the address below.

In-App Account Deletion (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account and all associated data is directly within the Sakli Kelime app:

  1. Open the Sakli Kelime app and sign in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the Settings or Profile menu.
  3. Tap on the Delete Account option and confirm your decision.

This will instantly and permanently delete your account, display name, gameplay history, and social records.

Alternative: Deletion request email

If you cannot access the app, you can send an email to: cioflexdev@gmail.com

What to include in your email request

  • Your display name
  • The email address linked to the account, if available
  • Any additional information that helps identify the correct account

Data that may be deleted

  • Account records
  • Profile and display name information
  • Gameplay history, scores, and archive records
  • Friend, social, and duel-related records associated with the account

How the process works

  1. Send an email request to cioflexdev@gmail.com.
  2. Include the identifying details listed above.
  3. If needed, additional verification information may be requested before deletion is completed.

Guest and local sessions

Guest or local-only sessions may be removed by clearing app data on the device. Server-linked accounts require the deletion request flow on this page.
