Account Deletion

Permanently remove your account.

If you created a DOPAMINE! account, you may request deletion of your account and associated data.

Last updated: June 13, 2026 Contact: cioflexdev@gmail.com

How to Delete Your Account

If account deletion is available in your current app version, you can start the deletion process from inside the app through the profile or settings area. If you cannot access that option, contact cioflexdev@gmail.com with the subject line Account Deletion Request.

Information to Include

  • your username
  • the email linked to your account
  • a short confirmation that you want permanent deletion

What Deletion Removes

  • account profile data
  • stored gameplay progress
  • wallet and progression data
  • order and delivery simulation history
  • saved account-linked settings

What May Be Retained

  • records required for legal compliance
  • fraud prevention or security investigation records
  • limited accounting or transaction records where required by law

Processing Time

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days unless a different period is required by law.

Important Note

Account deletion is permanent once completed, and deleted progress may not be recoverable.
