Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Yusuf Akyurt operates the DOPAMINE! mobile application and related website pages under the CioflexLabs brand. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we share it, and what choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • username
  • email address
  • authentication data handled by our authentication providers

Gameplay and Profile Data

  • progress, levels, rewards, orders, delivery history, wallet data, and settings
  • investment, mission, leaderboard, and progression data

Address and Location Data

  • selected address label
  • map selection data
  • precise location, if you grant permission

Location is used to help users select an in-app address and support map-based gameplay features.

Purchase Information

We use RevenueCat and App Store in-app purchases for consumable top-ups. We do not receive your full payment card details, but we may receive purchase status, product identifiers, and transaction-related records needed to credit in-app currency and support purchase restoration or verification.

Notifications

  • push token
  • notification preferences

Advertising and Device Data

If advertising features are active, third-party advertising partners may process device identifiers and advertising-related data to deliver or measure ads.

2. How We Use Information

  • create and manage accounts
  • save and sync gameplay progress
  • support wallet, courier, address, and investment systems
  • verify and apply consumable purchases
  • send optional notifications
  • maintain security, stability, and abuse prevention
  • respond to support and privacy requests

3. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate the app, including Supabase, RevenueCat, push notification providers, mapping/geocoding services, App Store infrastructure, and advertising providers if ads are enabled.

4. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain account progress, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize data unless retention is required for security, fraud prevention, accounting, or legal compliance.

5. Your Rights

  • access your personal data
  • request correction of inaccurate data
  • request account deletion
  • withdraw optional permissions such as notifications or location

For privacy or data requests, contact cioflexdev@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

DOPAMINE! is not intended for children in violation of applicable law. If you believe a child has provided data unlawfully, contact us at cioflexdev@gmail.com.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date above.
